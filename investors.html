The opportunity

A structural gap that
technology can close.

Rural credit exclusion is systemic

Only 22.7% of adults in rural Colombian municipalities accessed any formal credit product in 2023 — compared to near-universal access in cities. Smallholder farmers are not excluded because their businesses are unviable. They are excluded because the due diligence cost per small loan is prohibitive for traditional banks.

At the same time, impact investors want exposure to Colombian agricultural SMEs. The returns are competitive, the ESG narrative is strong, and the demand from entrepreneurs is massive. The problem is that there is no scalable, cost-effective way to source and verify investment-grade opportunities in the rural market.

Seeds closes both gaps simultaneously

AI replaces the field agent. One Seeds AI assistant can process 500 conversations simultaneously. One human field agent processes 5 per day. This is the structural cost advantage that makes rural agricultural finance economically viable at scale.

Business model

Four revenue streams.
Entrepreneurs always free.

Seeds operates as a networking platform — never holding investor money directly. A licensed fiduciaria holds escrow. Seeds earns from facilitating the connection.

Primary · scales with volume

Transaction fee

1.5–2% charged to the investor when capital is successfully placed. Scales directly with deal volume with zero marginal cost.

Recurring · institutional

SaaS subscription

Monthly fee for institutional investors accessing the full deal pipeline API, due diligence reports, and ESG impact documentation.

Servicing fee
